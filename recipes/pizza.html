Pizza

Description

A mouthwatering pizza, freshly baked to perfection, features a tantalizing medley of flavors and textures. Its golden crust, crispy on the edges and soft in the center, supports a generous layer of tangy tomato sauce, adorned with a rich blanket of melted mozzarella cheese. Vibrant toppings such as savory pepperoni, earthy mushrooms, and crisp bell peppers dance across its surface, promising a delightful explosion of taste with every bite.

ingredients

  • active dry yeast
  • all-purpose flour
  • olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Desired toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients
  2. Knead dough until smooth
  3. Let dough rise
  4. Roll out dough into a circle
  5. Spread pizza sauce over dough
  6. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top
  7. Add desired toppings
  8. Bake in preheated oven
  9. Slice and enjoy!
