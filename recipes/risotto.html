Risotto

Description

\

Indulge in the luxurious simplicity of a classic risotto, a creamy Italian rice dish that captivates with its velvety texture and depth of flavor. Arborio rice, gently simmered in a flavorful broth, absorbs the essence of aromatic ingredients like onions, garlic, and white wine. Parmesan cheese adds a rich, nutty undertone while butter lends a luscious finish. Each spoonful is a symphony of comfort and sophistication, inviting you to savor the timeless elegance of Italian cuisine.

ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • chicken or vegetable broth
  • butter
  • onion
  • garlic
  • white wine
  • parmesan
  • parsley
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Heat chicken or vegetable broth in a saucepan and keep it warm
  2. Melt butter in a separate pan and saut√© finely chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent
  3. Add Arborio rice and stir until lightly toasted
  4. Pour in white wine and cook until absorbed
  5. Gradually add warm broth, stirring frequently, until rice is creamy and tender, about 18-20 minutes
  6. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper to taste
  7. Garnish with parsley if desired
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your creamy risotto!
