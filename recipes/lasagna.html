Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the rich layers of lasagna: tender pasta, creamy ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, all harmonized with savory marinara sauce and ground meat. Baked to perfection, it's a comforting Italian classic, inviting you to savor every bite.

ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • marinara sauce
  • ground beef (or substitute)
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  2. Brown ground beef and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning
  3. Mix ricotta cheese with grated Parmesan cheese
  4. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese
  5. Repeat layers
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
  7. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until cheese is bubbly
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving
  9. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
